Cancer is Not a Death Sentence — It’s a Journey of Hope!

Welcome to Sprout and Live Hope Foundation, a safe space for emotional healing and mental wellness for cancer patients and survivors. Whether you're newly diagnosed, in treatment, or in remission — you are not alone. Together, we sprout hope and live fully.

~ Teresah Wanjiku, Cancer Survivor & Mental Health Advocate

Our Services

  • Counseling Programs: Tailored emotional support for cancer patients and caregivers
  • Support Groups: Safe, guided peer-to-peer sharing and healing sessions
  • Community Outreach: Reaching underserved communities with mental health resources
  • Workshops & Events: Educational and motivational sessions for mental resilience

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide compassionate mental health support and foster a nurturing community for cancer patients and survivors, promoting resilience, hope, and holistic healing.

Our Vision

A world where cancer patients and survivors are empowered to thrive emotionally, mentally, and physically.
